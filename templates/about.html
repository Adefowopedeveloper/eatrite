About Us

At EATRITE, we serve not just food but an experience. Discover the passion behind our flavors!

Our Story

Since its founding in 2015, EATRITE has been dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience that caters to all taste preferences. Founded by visionary Nigerian entrepreneur, Adefowope Fowosire, EATRITE was born out of a passion for creating delicious, high-quality meals that bring people together to celebrate the joy of eating.

Our Commitment to Quality

At EATRITE, we believe food is more than just nourishment—it’s an experience to be savored, enjoyed, and shared. Our chefs work hard to ensure each ingredient shines, combining local and global flavors to create unforgettable dishes.

Our Founder

Our founder, Adefowope Fowosire, believes in the power of food to bring people together from all walks of life. His vision for EATRITE is simple: to provide high-quality, delicious meals that cater to every palate, all while creating a welcoming environment for our guests.

Join Us Today!

Join us at EATRITE for a dining experience that will leave you wanting more. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a casual dinner with friends, or a special celebration, we promise to deliver a meal that’s as satisfying as it is flavorful. Experience the EATRITE difference for yourself!
